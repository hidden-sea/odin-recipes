Raspberry and Blackberry Smoothie

A delightful smoothie that will quench anyone's thirst

Ingredients

  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup of blackberries
  • 1 cup of fresh raspberries
  • 1 6 ounce container vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 4 ice cubes

Directions

  1. Place all of the ingredients in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth like a smoothie
