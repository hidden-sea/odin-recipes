Mac and Cheese

Mac and cheese a classic comfort

Ingredients

  • macaroni
  • butter
  • flour
  • milk
  • cheese
    • cheddar
    • parmesean
  • seasoning
    • salt
    • pepper
    • paprika
  • bread crumbs

Directions

  1. Boil the noodles: Boil the macaroni in salted water until the noodles are al dente. Drain and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce: melt butter, then whisk in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over noodles and stir.
  3. Make the topping: Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs and toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread the topping over macaronie and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese: Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown.
